Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our GST billing service:

  • Business information (name, address, GSTIN) you provide
  • Invoice, customer, purchase, and expense data you enter
  • Device advertising ID (for serving ads)
  • Crash and usage analytics data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. When you opt for cloud backup, data is transmitted securely to our servers.

We use the advertising ID to serve relevant ads in the free version of the app. No personal or business data is shared with advertisers.

3. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data is stored locally using encrypted Hive databases. Cloud backups use TLS encryption in transit. You can delete all local data at any time by clearing app storage.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your cloud-stored data by contacting us on WhatsApp. You may also disable data collection by uninstalling the app.
